Stop AAPI Hate.org

Due to the alarming escalation in xenophobic and bigotry caused by the covid-19 pandemic, the Asian Pacific planning and policy council(a3pcon), Chine for Affirmative Action (CAA), and the Asian American Studies Department of San Francisco State University launched the Stop AAPI Hate reporting center on March 19, 2020. You can report any form of racism or harrasement you see to them and they will track and respond to the incidence. You can also donate to help there cause, it goes directly to the efforts to track down and respond to these incidents.

Self Evident Podcast

Self Evident is a podcat where they tell asian america's stories to explore what it really means to claim america today. Each episode presents an in-depth audio documentary or radically open conversations from asian american communites, across generations, across cultures, across class.
